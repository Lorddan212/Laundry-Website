How work moves

Our service flow is simple because customers care about speed, safety, and no mix-ups.

We keep the process practical and transparent, from pickup confirmation to delivery return.

1. Pickup is booked

We confirm the date, time window, area, and service type before dispatching pickup.

2. Items are logged

Orders are grouped, tagged, and marked for washing, ironing, or dry cleaning.

3. Cleaning starts

Loads move through the right handling path based on garment type and instructions.

4. Delivery is arranged

Finished orders are folded or hung, packaged, and returned within the agreed window.
