Quick answers

Common questions before booking

These are the details most new customers ask about before their first pickup.

How early should I book?

Same-day slots are limited. Booking at least one day ahead gives the best chance of getting your preferred window.

When do I get my clothes back?

Most regular laundry returns within 48 hours. Dry cleaning and bulky household items may take a little longer.

Can I request stain treatment?

Yes. Mention it in the order notes so the team can assess it before processing and advise if extra care is needed.

Do you handle apartment or business linen?

Yes, for light to medium recurring volume. We recommend calling or sending a WhatsApp message first for planning.
